Engineering Philosophy

Composable Architecture

I advocate for loosely coupled, highly cohesive systems. Whether microservices or modular monoliths, the goal is always independent deployability and clear domain boundaries (DDD).

Empowered Leadership

Technology is built by people. I focus on servant leadership—mentoring engineers through structured OKRs, career frameworks, and creating a culture of psychological safety.

Resilience by Design

Hope is not a strategy. I architect for failure using patterns like Circuit Breakers, Bulkheads, and comprehensive Observability (Logging, Metrics, Tracing) to ensure 99.9% uptime.
